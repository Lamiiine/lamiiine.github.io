Tech Blogs & resources I like

  • My favourite Cpp blogger is by far Jason Turner and he, also, has a Youtube channel where he talks about Cpp as well.
  • If you are interested in learning about ML, Data Science, and more then Sentdex is your guy. I leanred a great deal from him on debugging and problem solving with his excellent live coding videos.
  • Another interesting youtube channel I am addicted to is george hotz archive. George’s live coding videos, sometimes, go up to 10 hours, where he picks a topic he has little knowledge in and starts digging, learning about it and then implementing it and you will get to see the whole process. Just mind blowing.
