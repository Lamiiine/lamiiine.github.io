Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Algeria Startup Map. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Analytics and Usage Data

We use PostHog, a third-party analytics service, to understand how our website is used and to improve user experience. This includes:

  • Session recordings: We record user interactions including clicks, scrolls, and navigation patterns
  • Page views and navigation: Which pages you visit and how you navigate through our site
  • Device and browser information: Your browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device type
  • Location data: General geographic location (country/city level) based on IP address
  • Performance data: Page load times and technical performance metrics

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP addresses (anonymized after processing)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Referrer information (which site brought you to us)
  • Timestamps of visits and interactions

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Passwords or sensitive form data
  • Financial information
  • Content of private communications

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns and popular content
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand which startups and features are most popular
  • Optimize website performance and loading times
  • Generate anonymous statistical reports

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 PostHog Analytics

We use PostHog (posthog.com) for analytics and session replay. PostHog may process your data in accordance with their privacy policy. Key points:

  • Data is processed in the United States
  • PostHog implements industry-standard security measures
  • Data is used solely for analytics purposes
  • You can learn more at: PostHog Privacy Policy

4.2 No Data Sales

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies:

  • Analytics cookies: To track website usage and performance
  • Functional cookies: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Session storage: To maintain site functionality during your visit

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect website functionality.

6. Data Retention

  • Session recordings: Retained for up to 30 days
  • Analytics data: Aggregated data may be retained longer for trend analysis
  • Technical logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and performance monitoring

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking by rejecting cookies
  • Access: Request information about data we've collected
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data transmission over HTTPS encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Anonymous data processing where possible

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Algeria, including the United States where PostHog processes data. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Data Controller: Website Administrator

For specific data requests or privacy concerns, please reach out through our website or the contact methods available on our main page.
